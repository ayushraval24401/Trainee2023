Fashion Week

The podium of fashion can not only be used for displaying clothes but also to reach out common masses. Fashion show event theme can be effectively used to create awareness among common people on any social issue. Through fashion show, designers can pick any cause that they want to send to the public. Big fashion events are covered by leading news media. Media is the best medium to reach the common public. Media telecast the event on their channels and also publish about the event on newspapers. Thus, fashion shows are not all about chic clothes and glamorous models.
